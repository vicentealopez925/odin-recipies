Spaogeti

Look at that al dente

Ingredients

  • Spaogeti Syrup
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ground Turkey
    • Steps
    1. Boil 4 cups of water
    2. Open your bag of spaogeti syrup
    3. Place the water into the syrup
    4. boil until al dente
    5. Drain the noodles with a strainer or drainer
    6. plate with your favorite parmasean
